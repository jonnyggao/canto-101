　Unit 15 Reflections　


Presentation


Each participant needs to prepare a short presentation (about 5 minutes) on the topic that they are interested in. The following are some suggestions.


  1. Your favourite place in Hong Kong

  2. Your favourite food.

  3. Your feelings about working or studying in HKIEd.

  4. Your reflection on this Cantonese course.

  5. Any interesting or embarrassing things which have happened when you communicated with people in Cantonese.

< You are welcome to choose your own topics >

Sharing


Can you briefly share with each other your feelings about studying this Cantonese course?
