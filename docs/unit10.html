　Unit 10 Useful Educational Terms　


Objectives


  1. To recognize and pronounce Cantonese words with the initials [g], [j], [h], [k], [f];
  2. To recognize and pronounce Cantonese words with the finals [o], [i], [aan], [ei], [au];
  3. To use simple Cantonese vocabulary and expressions for educational terms..




Warm up　　 Work together.


  • How do you come to the campus?
  • Which form of transport do you like most?




Vocabularies





Sentence structure


Choice – type question (S Adj a3?)


When do we have the test?.

我哋幾時要測驗呀？

[ngo5 dei6 gei2 si4 yiu3 chak1 yim6 a3?]


Do you have the notes and the time table of the first semester?

你有冇筆記同上學期時間表呀？

[nei5 yau5 mou5 bat1 gei3 tung4 seung6 hok6 kei1 si4 gaan3 biu2 a3?]


Which subjects do you study, arts or science?

你讀文科定理科㗎？

[nei5 duk6 man4 fo1 ding6 lei5 fo1 ga3?]




Conversation　　 Practice with a partner.


Scene: Sally and Linda are chatting in the classroom.


Sally: When do we have the test?.

　　　我哋幾時要測驗呀？

　　　[ngo5 dei6 gei2 si4 yiu3 chak1 yim6 a3?]


Linda: Next Thursday.

　　　下個星期四。

　　　 [ha6 go3 sing1 kei4 sei3.]


Sally: Do you have the notes and the time table of the first semester?

　　　 你有冇筆記同上學期時間表呀？

　　　 [nei5 yau5 mou5 bat1 gei3 tung4 seung5 hok6 kei1 si4 gaan3 biu2 a3?]


Linda: Yes, the lecturer gave them out in the tutorial lesson.

　　　 有，喺上導修課時講師派㗎。

　　　 [yau5, hai2 seung6 dou6 sau1 fo3 si4 gong2 si1 paai3 ga3.]


Sally: Please lend them to me to photocopy.

　　　你借俾我影印吖。

　　　[nei5 je3 bei2 ngo5 ying2 yan3 a1]




Quiz


  1. Which subjects do you teach in HKIEd, arts or science?

  2. When did you join HKIEd?

  3. Have you taken any notes in the lessons?
