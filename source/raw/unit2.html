Unit 2 Greetings　


Objectives


  1. To recognize and pronounce Cantonese words with the initials [j], [h], [s], [ng], [g];
  2. To recognize and pronounce Cantonese words with the finals [a], [ai], [an], [aan], [ang];
  3. To use simple Cantonese vocabulary and expressions in greetings.




Warm up 　　Work together.


  • What lesson are you having right now?
  • Can you say ‘maan5 on1’ to your teacher and classmates?




Vocabulary 　　Read the words in Cantonese together.






Sentence structure


Subject – Verb – Object Sentences (S V O)


I am English.

我係英國人。

[ngo5 hai6 ying1 gwok3 yan4.]


I go to the library.

我去圖書館。

[ngo5 heui3 tou4 syu1 gun2.]


I am heading back home.

我返屋企。

[ngo5 faan1 uk1 kei2.]




Conversation 　　Practice with a partner.


Scene I: Making a new friend.


Sally: I am Sally. What is your name?

　　　我係Sally，你叫咩名呀？

　　　[ngo5 hai6 Sally, nei5 giu3 mei1 meng2 a3?]


Linda: My name is Linda. Nice to meet you.

　　　我叫Linda。好高興認識你。

　　　[ngo5 giu3 Linda, hou2 gou1 hing3 ying6 sik1 nei5.]


Sally: Where do you come from?

　　　你係邊度人呀？

　　　[nei5 hai6 bin1 dou6 yan4 a3?]


Linda: I am English.

　　　我係英國人。

　　　[ngo5 hai6 ying1 gwok3 yan4.]


Sally: I come from USA.

　　　我喺美國嚟嘅。

　　　[ngo5 hai2 mei5 gwok3 lei4 ge3.]


Linda: We are in the same class learning Cantonese.

　　　我哋同一班上廣州話堂呀！

　　　 [ngo5 gei6 tung4 yat1 baan1 seung5 gwong2 jau1 wa5 tong4 a3!]


Scene II: Peter meets John on campus.


Peter: Good evening, John.

　　　晚安，John.

　　　[maan5 on1, John. ]


John: Good evening, Peter

　　　 晚安, Peter.

　　　 [maan5 on1, Peter.]


Peter: How are you?

　　　你好嗎？

　　　[nei5 hou2 ma1?]


John: I am fine. And you?

　　　我幾好。你呢？

　　　[ngo5 gei2 hou2. nei5 ne1?]


Peter: Not too bad. But I am very busy recently.

　　　仲可以，但係最近好忙。

　　　[jung6 ho2 yi5. daan6 hai6 jeui3 gan6 hou2 mong4.]


John: Where are you going?

　　　 你而家去邊度？

　　　 [nei5 yi4 ga1 heui3 bin1 do6?]


Peter: I am going to the library.

　　　我去圖書館。

　　　[ngo5 heui3 tou4 syu1 gun2.]


John: I am heading back home. Goodbye.

　　　 我返屋企喇，再見。

　　　 [ngo5 faan1 uk1 kei2 la3, joi3 gin3.]




Discussion　　 Ask your partner(s) these questions. Ask follow-up questions!


1.　How do you say ‘teacher’ in Cantonese? Can you greet your teacher properly?


2.　Can you say the sentence ‘How are you?’ in Cantonese?
